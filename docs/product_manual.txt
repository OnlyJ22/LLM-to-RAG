Product Overview
The enterprise platform supports up to 10000 concurrent users.
The platform is available on Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems.

Installation Requirements
Minimum 8GB RAM required for standard installation.
32GB RAM recommended for enterprise deployments.
At least 50GB of free disk space required.

Troubleshooting
If the application fails to start, restart the service from the admin panel.
Connection errors are most commonly caused by firewall settings blocking port 8080.
Contact technical support at techsupport@company.com for unresolved issues.

Licensing
Standard licenses support up to 25 users.
Enterprise licenses support unlimited users.
License keys must be renewed annually before expiration.
