Employee Onboarding Policy
New employees must complete onboarding within their first 5 days.
All new hires must submit identification documents to HR within 24 hours of start date.

Time Off Policy
Full time employees receive 15 days of paid time off per year.
Part time employees receive 7 days of paid time off per year.
Time off requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
Sick leave is separate from paid time off and allows up to 10 days per year.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Remote workers must be available during core hours of 9am to 3pm.
All remote work must be conducted on company approved devices.

Performance Review Policy
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year in June and December.
Employees must complete self assessments before each review.
Salary adjustments are determined during the December review cycle.
